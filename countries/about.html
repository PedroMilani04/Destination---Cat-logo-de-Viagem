The Project

DESTINATION tries to provide a brief, yet beautiful and intriguing view of our world.

This project was made to practice and enhance my abilities in HTML5 and CSS3, as well as English, design ideas and general notions. In this project, i tried my best to apply these ideas and introduce a passion that has come a long way with me: the passion for our world, along with the places, cultures and history surrounding us.

I hope to successfully show how i improved my skills as a developer and imprint my ideas and toughts in a pleasant, dynamic and efficient way.

Thank you for your time! Enjoy!
